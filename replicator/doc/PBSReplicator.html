    PBSReplicator (replicator v0.1.0)

    Why cook when you can use a replicator! Sadly this module can't provide a real replicator, but it can at least give you a simulated digital one. üôÇ

    A Virtual Star Trek-style Replicator

    Replicators have a charge that gets exhausted by replicating food. Replicators can be recharged.

    All replicators share a single menu which is accessible and updatable via functions.

    replicate(replicator, arg)

    Make some food from the menu.

    Parammeters:

    • replicator - The replicator to use.
    • {name, count} - A Tuple of the name of the item to make and the number of items (default: 1).

    Returns

    Returns the Tuple {:ok, replicator, food} if successful, {:error, reason} if fails.

    Sample: 

    iex> {:ok, rep, food} = PBSReplicator.replicate(rep, {:tako, 3})
{:ok, %PBSReplicator{charge: 198, ...]}, "üêôüêôüêô"}
